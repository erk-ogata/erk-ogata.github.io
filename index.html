Research Overview

Radiation Hydrodynamics Simulations

Simulations resolving gas inflows from the Bondi radius to the vicinity of the accretion disk, using the SFUMATO-M1 radiation hydrodynamics code.

Cosmological Simulations

Modeling galaxy formation and black hole growth in a cosmological context to connect small-scale physics with large-scale structure.

Line-Intensity Mapping

Developing mock line-intensity maps to study black hole signatures in recombination-line emission.
